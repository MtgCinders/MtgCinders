MTG Cinders

Cinders is a new Magic: the Gathering format that was made for people who enjoy playing Commander but would like to limit the format's card pool and power level. Made to challenge people to brew the best deck they can with limited resources and the addition of a second commander.

The Deck

In Cinders you pick 2 legendary creatures as your commanders. This is done to increase the available choices for players when creating a new deck. The deck construction in Cinders follows all the Commander deck construction rules except:

  • 2 legendary creatures as commanders
  • 58 other cards
  • Up to 15 cards in your sideboard
  • Both commanders are treated as though they had partner
  • Only one copy of any card, except for basic lands
  • Abilities that bring cards from outside the game, can only take cards from your sideboard

The Game

Cinders follows all the Commander play rules except:

  • 3-6 players
  • Players begin the game with 30 life
  • There is no commander damage

Card Pool

All the standard cards (core sets, expansion sets and welcome decks) that have been printed from Magic Origins and onward.

Ban List

  • Baral, Chief of Compliance
  • Emrakul, the Promised End
  • Nexus of Fate
  • Reflector Mage
  • Sorcerous Spyglass
