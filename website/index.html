MTG Cinders

Cinders is new Magic: the Gathering format that was made for people who enjoyed playing the Commander format but would like to limit the card pool and the potential power level of the format.

The Deck

In Cinders you pick 2 legendary creatures as your commanders, this is done so that you have a lot of choices when picking commanders for your color combination.

  • 2 legendary creatures as commanders
  • 58 other cards
  • Both commanders are treated as though they had partner
  • Only one copy of any card, except for basic lands

The Game

Cinders follows all the Commander play rules except:

  • 3-6 players
  • Players begin the game with 30 life
  • There is no commander damage

Card Pool

All the standard cards (core sets, expansion sets and welcome decks) that have been printed from Magic Origins and onward.

Ban List

  • Baral, Chief of Compliance
  • Emrakul, the Promised End
  • Nexus of Fate
  • Reflector Mage
  • Sorcerous Spyglass
